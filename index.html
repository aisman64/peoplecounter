The NIPT project

Non-Intrusive People Tracking (NIPT) is a "privacy-ensuring" method of tracking people statistics without cameras. We use Intel Galileo to collect, aggregate and analyze radio packages, and correlate information across beings in "realtime." The technology can be used as part of people tracking in smart cities, smart factory, smart building, fleet management, etc., where people can be tracked while retaining their privacy "uncompromised".
